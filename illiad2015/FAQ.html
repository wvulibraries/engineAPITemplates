What is ILLiad?

ILLiad is the electronic system you use to request an items from other libraries through Interlibrary Loan.

What is my ILLiad username and password?

You use your WVU MyID username and password to access ILLiad.

Why do I need a username and password to use ILLiad?

Your username and password allows us to give you secure access to information about your interlibrary loan requests. We can also edit your personal information such as your address or phone number.

Who do I contact if I have problems with or questions about ILLiad?

You should contact the Library at which you set up your ILLiad account:
